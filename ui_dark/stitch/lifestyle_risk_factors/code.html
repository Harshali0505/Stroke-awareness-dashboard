Insight: Education-Lifestyle Correlation

Analysis indicates a significant positive correlation between physical activity levels and stroke risk awareness. Interestingly, patients with a history of hypertension show the highest awareness levels, suggesting that clinical touchpoints are effective. However, a critical educational gap remains among active smokers and sedentary populations who have no prior medical diagnosis.
