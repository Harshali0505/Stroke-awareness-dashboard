Leverage Clinical Trust

Healthcare providers remain the most trusted source. Equipping GPs with standardized stroke educational kits could improve high-level awareness by 15%.

Optimize Visual Media

TV and Video formats show higher retention of the B.E.F.A.S.T. acronym compared to text-based educational materials among the 50+ demographic.

Social Network Gaps

While reach is high for social media, effectiveness is "Low." Messaging should focus on shareable infographics that emphasize immediate action steps.

Workplace Integration

Workplace wellness shows the lowest reach but consistent retention. Targeting HR departments for "Lunch & Learn" stroke workshops is a high-potential expansion area.
